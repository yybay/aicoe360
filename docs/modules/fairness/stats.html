valiant.fairness.samplers¶

This module builds sampling strategies that ensures balanced sampling across important key predictors, beyond the y-label.

For instance, for a classifier that has protected classes (e.g. gender) as predictor(s), it is imperative that during the sampling process of train_test_split, there exists apprximately balanced numbers of males and females in both the train and test set, in order for the model to be reliable, trustworthy, and devoid of potential biases.
